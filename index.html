Rooted in Tradition, Grown with Passion

Chanzo Coffee was born from a deep respect for Uganda's rich coffee heritage. "Chanzo," meaning "source" in Swahili, represents our commitment to connecting you directly to the origin of some of the world's most unique coffees. We partner with local farming communities, ensuring ethical practices and sustainable livelihoods, to bring you a product that is as rich in story as it is in flavor.

Our journey begins in the fertile volcanic soils of the Rwenzori Mountains and the slopes of Mount Elgon, where generations of farmers have perfected the art of coffee cultivation. We believe in preserving this legacy and sharing it with the world.

The Pearl of Africa's Finest Beans

Uganda's unique climate and geography create a perfect environment for growing world-class coffee. We offer both Arabica and Robusta varieties, each with a distinct and memorable character.

Single-Origin Arabica

Grown at high altitudes, our Arabica beans are known for their bright, complex acidity and nuanced flavor profiles. Expect notes of citrus, berry, and dark chocolate with a clean, winey finish. Ideal for specialty coffee markets seeking a premium, aromatic experience.

  • Region: Mt. Elgon, Rwenzori Mountains
  • Altitude: 1,500 - 2,300 meters
  • Process: Washed & Natural
  • Tasting Notes: Floral, Fruity, Bright

Robust Robusta

Uganda is the birthplace of Robusta, and our beans are a testament to this powerful legacy. Full-bodied with a smooth, creamy mouthfeel and low acidity. Its bold, earthy flavors of chocolate and spice make it a perfect base for espressos and blends, providing that sought-after caffeine kick.

  • Region: Lake Victoria Basin
  • Altitude: 900 - 1,500 meters
  • Process: Natural
  • Tasting Notes: Bold, Chocolatey, Nutty
